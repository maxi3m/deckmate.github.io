Why HackerBatch?

01

High Standards

Instantly get matched with top hackers and companies that are pre-screened and interviewed. We don't just let anyone join our hiring pool.

02

No Recruiting Agencies

HackerBatch isn't a recruiting agency. Unlike recruiters we curate your application into 'batches' for hiring season and job type and send them out to companies. After that it is up to the startup/company to reach out if they see a good fit.

03

Easy to Use

HackerBatch applying to internships as easy as buying ticket to your favourite concert.

04

Direct Contact

HackerBatch directly connects interns & startups through our simple, powerful & elegant matching platform.

How it Works

Step 1 Step 2 Step 3 Step 4 Step 5 Step 6

Hacker applies online at HackerBatch.

Hacker is sent and passes skill testing questions.

Hacker passes human and automated screening.

Hacker placed into 'batch' (selected by experience, start date, etc) and is given access to resources (i.e. interview workshops/tech talks)

'Batch' of hackers is sent out to Company at the end of the month

Company reaches out to Hacker and offers interview.
